## 📌 Project Overview
Exploratory Data Analysis on FedEx logistics shipment data
for medical and pharmaceutical products across Africa (2006–2015).
Goal: Find patterns in delivery time, freight cost, and shipment
volume to improve logistics performance.

## 📈 Key Findings
- Total shipment value: **$1,627.6M** over 9 years
- Average delivery time: **105.6 days** — significant delay opportunity
- Freight cost dropped from **$47.52/kg (2006)** to **$9.24/kg (2015)**
- **South Africa** had the highest shipment volume (1,406 shipments)
- **ARV** product group takes longest to deliver — 108 days average
- Shipment volume peaked in **2014 at 947 shipments**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
1. Reduce delivery time for ARV products — 108 days is too high
2. Investigate 2015 volume drop from 947 to 355 shipments
3. Standardise Incoterms — N/A FROM RDC (5,404) dominates unusually
